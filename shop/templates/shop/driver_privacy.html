ZAYGO Captains Driver App Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ZAYGO Captains collects, uses, stores, and protects driver information when using the driver application and related services.

Applies to all drivers using the app
Covers account, orders, and app usage data

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information required to operate the driver service effectively, including:

  • Personal information such as name, phone number, profile photo, and identification details.
  • Vehicle information including type, plate number, and related registration details.
  • Location data while the app is in use to support delivery assignment, route tracking, and order updates.
  • Order and communication data such as assigned orders, delivery status, and in-app chat or call records where applicable.
  • Technical information such as device type, app version, IP address, and usage logs.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide, maintain, and improve the driver service. This includes:

  • Creating and managing driver accounts.
  • Assigning delivery tasks and tracking fulfillment.
  • Improving support, safety, fraud prevention, and system performance.
  • Sending service-related notifications such as order updates or account alerts.

3. Location Data

The driver app may use background and foreground location access to support live tracking, dispatch coordination, and proof of delivery operations. Location access is only used for operational needs related to the delivery service.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information when necessary to operate the service, including with:

  • Customers receiving their deliveries.
  • Stores or partners managing assigned delivery orders.
  • Service providers supporting hosting, analytics, communications, or technical operations.
  • Authorities when disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect rights and safety.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect driver data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

Driver information may be retained for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce operational policies.

7. Driver Rights

Depending on applicable law, drivers may request access to certain personal information, correction of inaccurate data, or deletion where permitted and operationally appropriate.

Questions about privacy requests or account data can be directed through the official support channel used by the platform.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the driver app after updates means the updated policy becomes effective from the date it is published.
